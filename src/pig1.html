The Three Little Pigs

The Straw

The first little pig was very lazy and did not like to work at all, so he decided to create his house out of straw



The little pig quickly built his house, then celebrated. He played, sang, and danced for the rest of the day.



The following day, a wolf stumbled upon the area where the little pigs lived. He saw the straw house and smelled the aromatic scent of the pig, which made his mouth water.



Luckily, the little pig was inside the house. The wolf knocked and said, "Little pig, let me in." The pig saw the wolf's big paws through the keyhole, so he answered, "No! Not by the hairs of my chinny chin chin!"



The wolf, a bit annoyed, said, "Then I'll huff and I'll puff and I'll blow your house down." So the wolf successfully blew the straw house down.



However, before he could enjoy his supper, the little pig swiftly ran away and hid with the second little pig.
